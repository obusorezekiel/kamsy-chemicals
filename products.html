Industrial Chemicals

Our industrial chemicals division provides high-purity chemicals essential for manufacturing and processing industries. We supply a comprehensive range of industrial-grade chemicals that meet international quality standards.

Our Industrial Chemical Products

  • Caustic Soda: High-purity sodium hydroxide (99% purity) for various industrial applications including pulp and paper, textiles, and water treatment.
  • Hydrochloric Acid: Concentrated HCl (32-35%) for metal processing, food processing, and chemical manufacturing.
  • Sulfuric Acid: High-grade sulfuric acid (98% concentration) for fertilizer production, metal processing, and chemical synthesis.
  • Hydrogen Peroxide: Oxidizing agent (30-50% solution) for bleaching, disinfection, and chemical processing applications.
  • Nitric Acid: Concentrated nitric acid for metal etching, fertilizer production, and chemical manufacturing.
  • Sodium Carbonate: Soda ash for glass manufacturing, detergent production, and water treatment.

Applications

Our industrial chemicals are used across various sectors including manufacturing, textiles, paper and pulp, water treatment, metal processing, and chemical synthesis industries.
