Our story

BUILDING A BETTER FUTURE

CREATING REALITY WITH WORKING TOGETHER

Kamsy Chemicals & Allied Products Limited is an indigenous chemical raw materials marketer and supplier. We are young but dynamic and innovative in our business endeavors as we are approaching the hallmark of a decade in business.

Kamsy Chemicals and Allied Products Limited was incorporated as a private limited liability company on April 29th, 2009. It was incorporated to carry on the business of importation and distribution (amongst others) of Chemical Raw Materials.

Virtually everything that adds value to human existence, comfort and pleasure are directly or indirectly produced from chemical raw materials to end user Industries/Manufacturers. Our company has been on the steady growth path from inception to date. We have won the confidence of our customers because we have made their business success our utmost priority as we maintained unbroken commitment to providing them with high grade chemical raw materials and prompt delivery services. Their success story is our motivation and stay in business.

OUR LEADERSHIP

Mr. Francis Ofurum, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Kamsy Chemicals & Allied Products Limited, the owners and full occupants of KAMSY PLACE, the home of Quality Chemicals & Reliable Services. He has close to two decades of experience in chemical marketing business. He was the General Manager (Chemical Marketing) in Linland Nigeria Limited, a subsidiary of Lin land Petrochemicals UK Limited. He holds a Master's degree in Business Administration and is an associate member of the Nigeria Institute of Management.

Our commitment

HEALTH, SAFETY & ENVIRONMENT (HSE)

Kamsy Chemicals & Allied Products Ltd is committed to protecting the health and safety of its workforce, involved in her operations as well as that of the customers, partners and visitors while minimizing damages caused to the environment by controlling the risk associated with our operations.

From inception to date, Kamsy Chemicals & Allied Products Ltd has continuously strengthened its commitments to develop a robust HSE culture and has improved its performance. Kamsy Chemicals & Allied Products Ltd adheres to international standards and benchmark in HSE.

How we operate

OUR BUSINESS OPERATING PRINCIPLES (BOP)

Kamsy Chemicals & Allied Products Ltd is committed to the following Business Operating Principles:

  • Ensuring that all our business activities are done with strict adherence to applicable government laws and regulatory requirements, particularly National Agency for Food and Drug Control – NAFDAC.
  • We are dedicated to efficient administration and customer/clients services ensuring their satisfaction.
  • We conduct our activities with full consciousness and regard for the environmental standards as well as the social and cultural sensitivities of the local communities in which we operate.
  • At all times, we underline our intention to undertake business activities according to established company procedures in a transparent manner, subject to audit and verifications.
  • Qualitative human workforce is a valuable competitive edge. We hire and retain qualified personnel available and maximize their opportunities for success through training and development.
