ABOUT US

OUR INVESTMENT ACROSS MULTIPLE SECTORS

Industrial Chemicals

Our operations focus on delivering high-quality industrial chemicals efficiently while serving the manufacturing and processing industries that rely on them. We provide coordinated, value-driven chemical solutions optimised for performance.

Specialty Chemicals

We invest in specialty chemical platforms that bring innovation together for meaningful applications, supporting well-curated solutions across pharmaceutical, agricultural, and industrial sectors.

Agrochemicals

Our agrochemical portfolio is centred on thoughtful development and product management, delivering and investing in practical solutions that meet market needs and create value for agricultural communities.

Paints & Coatings

We provide comprehensive paint and coating solutions that protect and enhance surfaces across various industries, delivering quality products that meet the highest standards of durability and performance.

Laboratory Chemicals

Our laboratory chemicals division focuses on providing high-purity reagents and analytical solutions for research institutions, healthcare facilities, and quality control laboratories.
